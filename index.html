Projects

Our party works on a variety of reforms and projects for our community. Below you can read about what we are planning to do in the next few years.

Haritha Haram

A massive afforestation program aimed at increasing the green cover of Telangana.

Mission Bhagiratha

Providing safe drinking water to every household in Telangana.

Power for All

Ensuring 24/7 electricity supply for residential, agricultural, and industrial needs.

Double Bedroom Housing

Providing free housing for the underprivileged across Telangana.

Rythu Bandhu

Direct financial support for farmers to encourage agriculture.

Mission Kakatiya

Restoring lakes and water bodies to improve irrigation facilities.

KCR Kits

Supporting pregnant women with healthcare and essential supplies.

TS-iPASS

A single-window clearance system to promote industrial growth.
